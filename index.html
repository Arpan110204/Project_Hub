FAQ

What is ProjectHub?

ProjectHub is a platform that connects students with innovative projects and research opportunities.

How do I join?

You can join by signing up through our website and applying for the projects you are interested in.

Are there any costs involved?

No, joining ProjectHub is completely free for students.

Can I submit my own projects?

Yes, you can submit your projects and showcase them to other students.

Who can view my projects?

Your projects can be viewed by other students and faculty members on the platform.

Is there a community support?

Yes, we have a community forum where you can ask questions and get support from other members.
